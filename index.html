ACADEMIC

BSBA Major in Human Resource

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Human Resource Development Management prepares the students for a career in Human Resource Management in various corporations whether in the manufacturing, marketing and service sectors, or in the different types of industries such as pharmaceutical, semiconductor, food and beverage, banking industries and other types of organizations.

Career Opportunities

1. Management Trainee in Human Resource
2. Administrative Assistant
3. Executive Assistant
4. Recruitment Assistant
5. Training and Development Staff
6. Labor Relations Staff


BSBA Major in Marketing Management

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Marketing Management prepares the students to be responsive to the total environment by providing technical skills and competencies in the areas of Marketing.

Career Opportunities

1. Management Trainee in Marketing
2. Marketing Assistant
3. Account Executive
4. Sales Associate
5. PR/Advertising Assistant
6. Events Management Associate
7. Product/Brand Assistant
8. Marketing Consultant


BSBA Major in Financial Management

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Financial Management prepares the students to possess a strong foundation on theory, principles and concepts, as well as analytical tools and perspectives that would provide a sound and competitive basis for financial and investment decision making.Complete Understanding of the concepts, Principles and the theories of financial management. Help the students to seek employment and assume entry-level jobs or positions of responsibility as Financial Analyst, Financial Manager or Executive. Prepare students to pursue a teaching career or graduate studies in business.

Career Opportunities

1. Credit Analyst
2. Treasury Assistant
3. Stock Analyst
4. Trust Management Staff
5. Credit and Collection Assistant
6. FX Trader


BSBA Major in Entrepreneurship

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Entrepreneurship program provides a strong foundation in entrepreneurship, basic business management and the mastery of the advancements in technology. The program also includes subjects in social sciences, mathematics and natural sciences. The program aims to produce graduates who are competent in generating business ideas, screening business opportunities, development and implementation of business plans, and maintaining the business enterprise. Prepare students to start and manage own business. Develop entrepreneurs who are motivated and knowledgeable in identifying opportunities. Develop and prepare students who can create highly feasible plan.

Career Opportunities

1. Mid-level management
2. Business consultant
3. Sales
4. Research and development
5. Not-for-profit fundraiser
6. Teacher
7. Recruiter
8. Business reporter
